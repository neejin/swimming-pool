Model & Methodology

Pass-through model: oil price change → CPI/PCE via distributed lag (peak effect at 6–9 months, fade by month 24). Elasticities: CPI 0.030 pp per 1% oil change, PCE 0.018 pp per 1% oil change. These are based on empirical literature estimates (IMF 2011; BIS 2012 working papers).

Baseline path: your forecast loaded from Google Sheets (month, cpi_baseline columns). Oil adjustment is layered on top — when oil = current price, projection = your baseline exactly. Shaded band = ±uncertainty (widens with horizon). Historical CPI from BLS via FRED (CPIAUCSL). This is a simplified projection tool — not a FOMC forecast.
