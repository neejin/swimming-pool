Model & Methodology

Pass-through model: oil price change → CPI/PCE via distributed lag (상승: peak 3-4개월, 6개월 내 완전 반영 | 하락: peak 5-6개월, 10개월 누적 · 75% 강도 (rockets & feathers)). Elasticities: CPI 0.030 pp per 1% oil change. 6-month distributed lag, weights sum to 1.0. These are based on empirical literature estimates (IMF 2011; BIS 2012 working papers).

Baseline path: your forecast loaded from Google Sheets (month, cpi_baseline columns). Past oil shock (shock_start_date 이후) 잔여 pass-through + 미래 시나리오 추가 효과를 분리 계산. Shaded band = ±uncertainty (widens with horizon). Historical CPI from BLS via FRED (CPIAUCSL). This is a simplified projection tool — not a FOMC forecast.
